Faith
  • Christianity

    • An overwhelming majority of Alabamians hold their faith to be of great importance, almost to a fault. Second only to Christianity is the devotion to football. Thus establishing a weekend routine that holds a steady devotedness that yet remains unmatched.

Football
  • High School

    • High School Football is a tried and true path towards local glory. Playing under the lights is a big-time asperation for the small time towns. With great fervor, young men practice and lay it out all on the line, if only to live imortally in the hearts and minds of the local populace.

  • College

    • Saturday's throughout the fall are littered with leaves drifting through the air as fans stream into their respective stadiums. Whether through the gates of Jordan Hare or Bryant-Denny, fans glean for the opportunity to come close to their idols. The boys of fall, the titans of the gridiron battle from start to finish, whilst in pursuit of a ring and a trophy.

Family
  • Community

    • Familial bonds carry a special weight that lasts not just through generations, but into friendships and beyond.
